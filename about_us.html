Quantum Aviation Services, why choose us? Quantum Aviation Services is an aircraft charter and management company drawing from decades of collective staff experience in the commercial aviation industry. Relatively small in size with two offices in the United Kingdom [London and East Midlands Airport] we aim to keep overheads low so that we maintain a stable business model, and for the client, be competitive on price.

We have no intention to grow to an unreasonable size as our experience tells us the bigger you get, the harder it is to maintain service levels. Our employees are commercial pilots, senior managers, and current commercial flight dispatchers.

From making your request you will have one point of contact who will walk you through the process and provide options to suit your preferences. We believe it is this personal touch that separates us from the competition.
