Global Coverage: Need a flight from Phnom pehn to London, Sydney to San Francisco, or Paris to Ibiza? Our international contacts work with us to ensure your trip goes well. We are connected.

Pricing: Our low cost business model allows us to find you the best private jet charter prices, ensuring your pricing are we offer is the most competitive in today’s market.

Sourcing of aircraft: Obviously with a flight from Sydney to San Francisco you would need an ultra long haul aircraft, Paris to Ibiza possibly a light jet. It’s the client’s choice. We shall always source you the cheapest options and provide expert advice for your needs, up to and including full size VVIP passenger aircraft if needed.

Safety Conscious: We only operate with aircraft that meet the exacting standards of the UK Civil Aviation Authority, the most highly regulating governing body in the world.

No Obligation: We are not a hard sell operator / no high pressure sales.

Empty Legs: When Quantum Aviation Services has a one-way charter, the aircraft is often without passengers when it repositions back to its base, or on to another global location. These are known as 'Empty Legs', and they are available at savings of up to 75% of the cost of a regular charter. Please view what “live” non passenger fights [empty legs] we have on offer today.
