VERY LIGHT JETS

Common Routes: London to Paris

MUSTANG
  • Seats: 4
  • Luggage: 2 Large Suitcases or 4 Carry on
  • Range: 2 hrs

The Citation Mustang is very popular within Europe, it is mainly used as an air taxi for short business/leisure trips because it is so cost effective, however It does not feature a toilet and can be cramped if loaded with 4 adults. For the price and convince the mustang is a great way to start your private jet experience and work your way up.

PHENOM 100
  • Seats: 5
  • Luggage: 4 Large Suitcases or 5 Carry on
  • Range: 2hrs 30mins

The Phenom 100 is the direct competitor to the Citation Mustang, the phenom 100E offers a more dynamic cabin which has been designed by BMW. Its more spacious and has a rear separate toilet which also couples as a 5th seat, this combined with a larger luggage hold and an overall more-luxury/sporty feel to the aircraft, this is definitely a private jet and not an air taxi.

Common Routes:

HAWKER 400
  • Seats: 7
  • Luggage: 7 Suitcases
  • Range: 3hrs

Thanks to the special and unique shape of the cabin it is the most comfortable plane in the light jets class. On board Hawker offers enough space for even 7 passengers. Flat floor makes it easier to move in the cabin. Hawker is recognized as the fastest in the class. You can reach your destination quicker and more efficiently.

CJ2
  • Seats: 6
  • Luggage: 10 Suitcases
  • Range: 3hrs

The Cessna Citation Jet CJ2 series are light business jets built by Cessna and part of the Citation family. A pressurised cabin accommodates a crew of two, up to six passengers and includes a toilet. Spaces for baggage are provided in the nose and tail-cone. This is a very comfortable and spacious jet for 5-6 passengers.

Common Routes:

CITATION XLS
  • Seats: 8
  • Luggage: 13 Suitcases
  • Range: 4hrs

The Citation XLS model is the most popular super light jet available for charter flights. Ideal for travelling around Europe without the need for a fuel stop. Seating up to eight people the cabin has a spacious feel to it, leather seats, a good size toilet and full bar.

PHENOM 300
  • Seats: 8
  • Luggage: 12 Suitcases
  • Range: 3hrs 30mins

The Phenom 300 is the competitor to the Citation XLS. It can accommodate up to eight passengers comfortably in a BMW designed interior. The executive jet from the Brazilian manufacturer Embraer is renowned for its speed. This is a very fast jet with a maximum speed of 453kt. The spacious baggage compartment makes this jet perfect for golfing and skiing trips.

MIDSIZE JET

Common Routes:

HAWKER 800
  • Seats: 8
  • Luggage: 8 Suitcases
  • Range: 4hrs 30mins

The Hawker 800 is one of the most successful private jets that British Aerospace has ever made. The cabin is typically configured with eight seats: a four-person club section, a three-person divan, and one forward-facing seat. The seats are fully adjustable and can swivel 360°. Also, there is a baggage compartment accessible in-flight.

CITATION VII
  • Seats: 8
  • Luggage: 7 Suitcases
  • Range: 4hrs

The Citation VII is a medium-sized business jet that provides a comfortable cabin for upto 8 passengers. The cabin incorporates reclining and 360 ° swivel chairs with adjustable headrests and footrests. The private lavatory compartment offers a vanity with a wash basin, a spacious countertop and generous storage space for its size.
